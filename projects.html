Raven

"Raven" was my first dive into game-making. With Unity and C#, I cooked up this 2D platformer starring Raven the cat. The player leaps between giant cat trees & moving cloud-platforms while collecting fish. I dreamed up and created this pixelated adventure myself. Games like "Celeste" and "Ori" inspired me to make a movement system that felt buttery, but with some mechanics that felt fun to learn and difficult to master. With the surge in popularity of Indie pixel-art games, I knew wanted to try my hand at creating some of my own sprites. That ambition led me to create every asset you see from scratch in Photo-shop. The trailer that accompanies this description was an assignment for an audio/video class in which we were tasked with making our first-ever (oftentimes janky) games look convincingly fun. After you watch it, you will see that "Raven" isn't just a game; it's the spark that lit my passion for digital creation.
