About

My name is Connor Kindall, & I am 2nd-year fast-track student at Boise State's GIMM program. GIMM is a lot more than your typical computer science program. Sure, we learn to code, but more importantly, we learn how to be adaptable. Much of the work in GIMM often feels like being told "here are the tools, figure out how to build the thing." I came into GIMM in 2022 with zero programming/digital art experience, and I will be leaving with the confidence to take on any project under any circumstance.

Outside of GIMM I am the VP of dev-club while juggling the responsibilities of working as a Certified Pharmacy Technician. I am Eager to apply my diverse & flexible skill set in a dynamic professional environment.
