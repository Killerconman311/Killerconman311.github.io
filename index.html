Connor Kindall

My name is Connor Kindall, and I am currently a fast-track student in my 2nd of three years at Boise State's Games, Interactive Media, & Mobile Technology and I currently serve as the Vice-President of Boise State's dev-club while working as a Certified/Immunizing Pharmacy Technician. Within the GIMM Program we are given a sort of digital toolbox of skills that includes 2D/3D/AR/VR development through Unity, Full stack web-development, proficient knowledge of the Adobe Suite, sound design through Audacity and Audition, physical computing by development on apps for Arduino, 3D modeling/texturing/rigging and animating through Maya and Substance Painter, and much more.
